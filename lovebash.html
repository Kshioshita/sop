Dear Bash,

From the very first moment I met you, you became a man that I couldn't forget. I always know where I'm at with you. You've helped me organize my life by teaching me to make directories and list those who have cluttered my past, even those who I've forgotten about and are invisible. Even when I wanted to grow and change you were by my side. When I wanted to move our relationship to the next level, you easily copied my lead. You've even learned to auto complete my sentences; or at least try your best. Every time we touch we add something new to our lives. Even getting to know your cat has allowed my to read you like a book. I hope I'll never have to remove you from my life!

Love,

Kaylin

  • man : shows manual page of command
  • pwd : shows what directory you're in
  • mkdir : make a new directory
  • ls : lists files and directories within present working directory
  • ls -la : lists files and directories including invisible dot files
  • cd : change directories
  • mv : move a file
  • cp : copy a file
  • tab : auto fill
  • tab tab : gives a list of possible matches
  • touch : creates file if filename doesn't exist
  • cat : read a file
  • rm : remove a file
